Apartment moving cost calculator: what to include

A move is not just rent. Most renters need cash for the first month, deposit, movers, supplies, utility setup, insurance and first-week essentials.

Use this guide with the free MoveBudget calculator.

1. Upfront housing costs

Start with security deposit, first month rent, application fees, admin fees, pet fees and any required building move-in fees.

2. Physical move costs

Add movers, truck rental, gas, tolls, parking, elevator reservations, packing supplies and tips. These costs vary by distance and building rules.

3. Setup and replacement costs

Include utilities, internet, renters insurance, cleaning supplies, furniture, kitchen basics and emergency cash for first-week surprises.

Checklist before you sign
