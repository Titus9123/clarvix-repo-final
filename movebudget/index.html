What to budget beyond rent

  • Deposit and first month rent are usually the biggest cash hit.
  • Movers, truck rental, fuel, parking and tips can change quickly.
  • Utility deposits, internet setup and renters insurance are easy to forget.
  • Furniture and basic household supplies still affect first-month cash.

MoveBudget FAQ

What should I include in a moving budget?

Include first month rent, security deposit, application/admin fees, movers or truck rental, packing supplies, utility setup, renters insurance, furniture essentials and a buffer.

Can I save the result?

Yes. You can copy the result, download a CSV, or print/save the page as a PDF. No signup is required.

Is this financial advice?

No. MoveBudget is an educational planning calculator. Use estimates and verify actual fees with your landlord, moving company and utility providers.

Is private data sent anywhere?

No. The calculator runs in your browser and does not ask for addresses, account numbers or rental application credentials.
