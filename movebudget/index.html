What to budget beyond rent

  • Deposit and first month rent are usually the biggest cash hit.
  • Movers, truck rental, fuel, parking and tips can change quickly by distance and building rules.
  • Utility deposits, internet setup and renters insurance are easy to forget.
  • Furniture and basic household supplies often arrive after the move, but still affect first-month cash.
