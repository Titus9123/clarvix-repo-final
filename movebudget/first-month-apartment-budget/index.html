First month apartment budget: how much cash do you need?

Your first month apartment budget is usually much larger than rent alone. A simple planning range is 2–4× monthly rent once deposit, fees, movers, utilities and essentials are included.

This is not financial advice. It is a planning checklist to avoid common move-in surprises.

2× rentlow-friction move
3× rentcommon renter budget
4× renthigher-cost move

What pushes the budget higher?

  • Large security deposit or broker/admin fees.
  • Long-distance movers or difficult building access.
  • New furniture, kitchen items and cleaning supplies.
  • Utility deposits, internet installation and insurance.
