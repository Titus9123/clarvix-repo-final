Free budget planner FAQ

What is the best way to use this planner?

Start with take-home income, list fixed bills first, add flexible spending categories, then enter debts and your realistic savings goal. Use the result as a planning worksheet, not as financial advice.

Should I download PDF or CSV?

Download CSV if you want to edit your budget in Excel, Google Sheets or Numbers. Use PDF/print if you want a clean record to review with a partner or keep offline.

What is debt snowball vs avalanche?

Snowball focuses on the smallest balance first for motivation. Avalanche focuses on the highest APR first to reduce interest cost. This tool orders debts by your selected method for planning only.

Is my data uploaded?

No. The calculation and exports run in your browser. Do not enter sensitive account numbers, passwords or private identifiers.
