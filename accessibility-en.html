Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Clarvix is committed to digital accessibility and to ensuring equal use of the website for all users, including people with disabilities. We work to meet the requirements of Israeli Standard IS 5568 and WCAG 2.0 Level AA.

What does IS 5568 cover?

IS 5568 is the official Israeli digital accessibility standard, based on WCAG 2.0 Level AA. It applies to business websites active in Israel and requires equal access for people with visual, auditory, motor and cognitive impairments.

Accessibility features in place

  • ✓Text size — Enlarge or shrink the font through the accessibility tool.
  • ✓High contrast — High-contrast mode for visual impairments.
  • ✓Pause animations — Stop every animation and transition on the site.
  • ✓Readable font — Switch to a plain Arial font with increased spacing.
  • ✓Link highlight — Visually emphasize every link on the page.
  • ✓Large cursor — Enlarge the cursor for easier use.
  • ✓Keyboard navigation — Navigate the entire site by keyboard alone.
  • ✓Skip to content — Quick link to the main content for assistive navigation.
  • ✓ARIA labels — All interactive elements include accessible descriptions.
  • ✓RTL direction — The Hebrew and Arabic interfaces support right-to-left.
  • ✓Image alternatives — Every image includes descriptive alt text.
  • ✓4 languages — The site is available in Hebrew, English, Spanish and Arabic.

Our accessibility interface

The accessibility button ♿ appears on the left side of every page. Activating it opens a menu with tools to personalize accessibility. It is also reachable by keyboard — press Tab until you land on it, then press Enter.

Where we are still improving

We are aware that some content in different languages may include accessibility challenges that have not yet been fully addressed. We work continually to improve accessibility and will update this statement as improvements ship.

Report an accessibility issue

If you encounter any accessibility issue on the site, please contact us. We will address your report within 7 business days.
