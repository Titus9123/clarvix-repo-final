# Clarvix Asset Lab Distribution Kit

Purpose: turn each free utility into useful, non-spammy distribution assets that can earn early visits, bookmarks, shares, and contextual backlinks.

## Distribution rules

- Lead with the free utility, not with Clarvix services.
- Use the most specific URL for the audience and search intent.
- Avoid mass-submitting the same copy everywhere. Rewrite the opener for each community.
- Do not claim financial, medical, legal, or nutrition advice.
- Track wins weekly: referral source, indexed URLs, impressions, clicks, and tool events.

## Weekly distribution checklist

1. Pick one asset and one long-tail page per day.
2. Share only where the page solves the exact discussion or resource gap.
3. Record each placement with: date, URL shared, channel, note, and expected query/intent.
4. After 7 days, check Search Console impressions/clicks for those URLs.
5. Keep pages that earn impressions or engagement; rewrite titles/sections for pages with no movement after indexing.
