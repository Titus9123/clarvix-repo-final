FAQ

Common questions

Do you need access to my accounts?
Not for the first scan. Verify Lite uses read-only access later if you approve it.
Will Clarvix change my campaigns or website?
No. Public Scan, Verify Lite and Monitor do not make changes. Control Tower prepares approved fixes only.
Is this a PPC agency?
No. Clarvix is a revenue leak control layer. It can work with your agency or internal team.
Do you guarantee more revenue?
No. We identify and prioritize leaks that may be hurting lead capture, tracking quality or ad efficiency.
