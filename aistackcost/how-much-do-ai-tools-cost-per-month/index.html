How much do AI tools cost per month?

A solo user might spend $20–$60 per month. A creator or operator can easily spend $80–$200. A small team can cross $300–$1,000 per month once seats and overlapping tools are included.

These are planning examples, not vendor pricing promises. Always check current vendor pages before buying or renewing.

$20–$60solo or student stack
$80–$200creator/operator stack
$300+small team stack

Why the monthly total grows

AI tools often overlap. A user may pay for a chat assistant, a coding tool, an image tool, a transcription tool, an automation tool and a meeting-notes tool. Each seems affordable alone; together they become a real software budget.

Simple budget rule

Keep tools that save time every week, improve work quality, or replace another paid subscription. Review tools that are rarely used, duplicate another tool, or were bought for a one-time experiment.
