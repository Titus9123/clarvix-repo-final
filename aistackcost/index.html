What to cancel first

Look for duplicate tools, tools used less than once per week, annual plans that no longer match your workflow, and seats assigned to inactive users.

Monthly vs annual

Annual discounts can be useful, but they hide lock-in. Compare annual savings against your confidence that you will still use the tool in six months.

Team stack hygiene

Teams should review owners, seats, billing emails, renewal dates and overlapping features before adding another AI subscription.

FAQ

How much should I spend on AI tools?

There is no universal number. A useful rule is to compare monthly cost with hours saved, output quality, and whether the tool replaces another subscription.

Does this page recommend specific tools?

No. Tool names are examples only. There are no official partnerships, no affiliate links and no endorsements in this MVP.

Can this be used for a team?

Yes. Use seats to estimate total monthly cost. For real procurement, also review security, privacy and internal approval requirements.
