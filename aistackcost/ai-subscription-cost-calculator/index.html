AI subscription cost calculator: what to include

Most AI-tool budgets look small one subscription at a time. The real cost appears when you add every paid tool, every seat, annual renewals and duplicate features across the stack.

Use this guide with the free AIStackCost calculator to estimate your monthly and annual AI subscription cost.

1. List every AI tool

Include chat assistants, coding tools, design tools, meeting-note apps, automation tools, browser extensions, image/video tools and research tools. If a tool renews automatically, add it.

2. Count seats, not just subscriptions

A $20 tool with five seats is not a $20 monthly cost. It is $100 per month and $1,200 per year. Team subscriptions are where AI budgets quietly expand.

3. Mark keep, maybe cancel or cancel

Do not cancel useful tools blindly. Start by marking tools that duplicate another paid tool, tools nobody used this month, and tools bought for experiments that already ended.
