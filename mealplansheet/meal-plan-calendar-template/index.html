Meal planning workflow

A useful meal plan is realistic, repeatable and easy to shop for. Start with household size, weekly schedule, cooking energy and ingredients you already have.

  • Pick two easy dinners and two leftover-friendly meals.
  • Group groceries by category before shopping.
  • Prep one grain, one protein and one sauce when possible.

Use the free generator

MealPlanSheet turns a weekly goal into meals, groceries and prep steps. Copy the result, download CSV, or print the page for the fridge.
