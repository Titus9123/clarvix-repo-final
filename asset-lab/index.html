Economics

The goal is ad revenue, not identity with a theme.

Every candidate must prove an economic path: organic traffic first, publisher revenue later, paid validation only if unit economics make sense.

Display ads

RPM model

1,000,000 pageviews × $10 RPM = $10,000/month. Higher-value topics can require less traffic.

Arbitrage gate

RPV must beat CPV

RPV = revenue per visit. CPV = cost per visit. Paid traffic scales only when RPV > CPV with margin.

Portfolio

Rotate, do not attach

If a topic has weak RPM, weak demand or brand-safety risk, it gets paused and replaced.
